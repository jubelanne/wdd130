Whether you're seeking a peaceful solo paddle or a fun-filled outing with friends and family, our hourly boat rentals cater to all preferences and skill levels.

Boat Options:

  • Kayaks: Single and tandem kayaks available.
  • Stand Up Paddleboards (SUPs): Perfect for a leisurely paddle along the water.
  • Canoes: Great for small groups or those looking for a traditional paddling experience.

Rates:

Boat Type Price (First Hour)
Single Kayak $15

Additional Information:

  • Equipment Included: Rental prices include paddles and personal flotation devices (PFDs) for each participant.

Guelph Lake Hourly Boat Rentals

Explore the natural beauty and serene waters of Guelph Lake with our hourly kayak, stand-up paddleboard (SUP), and canoe rentals. Perfect for outdoor enthusiasts, families, and groups of friends, our rentals offer a fun and adventurous way to enjoy the great outdoors.

Why Choose Us?

  • Affordable Rates: Enjoy an hour of paddling for just $19 (plus HST). Our competitive pricing ensures you get the best value for your outdoor adventure.
  • Convenient Location: Located just north of Guelph, Ontario, our rental station is easily accessible, making it a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.
  • Quality Equipment: We provide well-maintained kayaks, SUPs, and canoes, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience on the water.
  • Scenic Views: Guelph Lake is known for its picturesque landscapes, abundant wildlife, and tranquil environment. Paddle through clear waters, explore hidden coves, and take in the stunning views.
  • Suitable for All Skill Levels: Whether you’re a seasoned paddler or a beginner, our rentals cater to all experience levels. Our friendly staff is on hand to provide basic instructions and safety tips.
  • Flexible Rental Options: Perfect for solo adventurers, couples, families, and groups. Customize your experience with flexible rental durations to fit your schedule. Rates: $19 for the first hour (plus HST). Additional hours available at discounted rates.

Additional Information:

  • Operating Hours: Open daily from 9 AM to 7 PM (weather permitting)
  • Safety First: Life jackets and safety gear are provided with every rental. Our staff conducts a brief safety orientation before you hit the water.
  • Booking: Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking in advance, especially on weekends and holidays, to ensure availability.

Stand Up Paddle Board Rentals

Embark on a unique adventure along the picturesque Grand River with our stand up paddleboarding service, offering you the opportunity to see the river from a completely different perspective. Glide through its waters, immersing yourself in the beauty of nature while enjoying a thrilling yet serene experience.

As you navigate through the varying currents of the Grand River, our expertly crafted stand up paddleboards provide stability and ease of use, ensuring an enjoyable excursion for paddlers of all skill levels. Specifically designed with extra width for enhanced stability, even beginners can feel confident as they embark on their paddleboarding journey.

For those seeking an adrenaline rush, tackling fast-moving waters on the Grand River adds an exhilarating dimension to the experience. To navigate these currents safely and effectively, we recommend kneeling on the board to lower your center of gravity, providing greater stability and control as you traverse the river's dynamic landscape.

Why Choose Us

  • Expertly crafted stand up paddleboards for stability and ease of use.
  • Extra-wide boards for enhanced stability, ideal for beginners.
  • Experienced guides ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience.
  • Breathtaking views of the Grand River from a unique perspective.
  • Unforgettable moments and memories to last a lifetime.
